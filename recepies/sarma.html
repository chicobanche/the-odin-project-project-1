Sarma

Description

Sarma is tradional Turkish dish but is also made all around the balkan

It is meat and/or rice rolled in sour cabbage

Ingrideints

  • Sour cabbage
  • Cooking Oil
  • salt
  • Meat/Rice or both

Steps

  1. Pepare the sour cabbage
  2. Roll the cabbage with meat/rice
  3. add oil/salt
  4. Let it boil
  5. Serve it
